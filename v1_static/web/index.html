House of Maldorne

This is the home of the nonprofit retro online gaming association House of Maldorne

We play, host, build, develop and recover old and new retro online games, from muds to roguelikes.

If you like this kind of stuff, take a look to some of our hosted games:

Ciudad Capital

Original mud developed by the House of Maldorne members. Open to players and in active development.
Web: ciudadcapital.net
Web client: play.ciudadcapital.net
Telnet client: mud.ciudadcapital.net port 5010

Iluminado - Mud

The first fork from Reinos de Leyenda, run by Gestur, Lummen, Astaroth, etc. Recovered backup from October 2007, including player characters. Open to players.
Web client: play.ciudadcapital.net
Telnet client: mud.ciudadcapital.net port 5030

Reinos de Leyenda

The first version of the well known spanish mud, run by Paris, Oskuro, Osucaru, etc. Incomplete version (maybe late 2002)! We are looking for an earlier full backup. Open to players.
Web client: play.ciudadcapital.net
Telnet client: mud.ciudadcapital.net port 5020

Ancient Kingdoms

Fork from Reinos de Leyenda, recovered backup from October 2003. Open to players.
Web client: play.ciudadcapital.net
Telnet client: mud.ciudadcapital.net port 5040

Endor Mud

New game based in the Tolkien's legendarium, hosted by the House of Maldorne association.
Open to players.
Web client: play.ciudadcapital.net
Telnet client: mud.ciudadcapital.net port 5050
